What is Domain Name and Hosting?

A domain name is your brand identity over internet. It gives a unique name to your website. While hosting is storage space where you keep your files to show them on internet.

No, I don't provide Domain Name and Hosting. You can purchase one for your website. The good thing is that I can assist you to figure out the right needs for your Domain Name and Hosting. So that, you may not be overcharged.
